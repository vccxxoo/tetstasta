About Anonymous

Anonymous is an international collective organized as a decentralized group of activist and hacktivist individuals.

The group originated in 2003 on 4chan, representing a digitized collective network.

Members, known as anons, are sometimes recognized by Guy Fawkes masks inspired by V for Vendetta.

Public opinion varies widely, with supporters calling them digital activists and critics labeling them cyber vigilantes.
